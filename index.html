About Apple

Apple Inc. ( English : in Apple, Inc. is ) a famous American multinational technology companies , the Consumer Electric, computer software , and online services are designed, developed and sold. The company's hardware products include iPhone smartphones, iPad tablet computers, Mac personal computers, iPod portable media players, Apple Watch smartwatches, and Apple TV digital media players. Apple's software includes Mac OS and iOS operating systems, ITunes Media Player, Safari Web Browser, and iLife & iWorks Creative & Productivity Suite, as well as professional applications: Final Cut Pro , Logic Pro, and Excode. Their online services include iTunes Store, iOS App Store and Mac App Store, Apple Music and iCloud . In April 1986, Steve Jobs , Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne Wozniak formed Apple 1 to develop and sell Apple 1 personal computers. In January 1986, it was incorporated as "Apple Computer, Inc." Within a few years, Job and Wozniak hired a computer design worker. In 1980, Apple announced an IPO for sudden economic success. And over the next few years, Apple introduced some new computers with innovative graphical user interfaces , such as the 1974 Macintosh.Computer. Apple's marketing ads also garnered a lot of praise. However, the high cost of the product, and the limited software cause problems, as well as the power struggle of the executives. Wozniak left Apple in 1985, leaving the job with some other employees and forming his own company.
